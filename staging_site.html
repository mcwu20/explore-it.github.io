Creative Brief

Title

Hang Gliders

Project Summary

This site will provide some information on hang gliders. It will include a brief history, how a hang glider works, where to hang glide, and a mini quiz based on the information provided.

Audience Profile

This site is visually designed for children around elementary to middle school.

Perception and Tone

I expect the viewers to want to find out more about hang gliders. I want them to feel interested and amazed or excited.

Communication Strategy

I intend to have animations or graphics to support the text. I will also try to minimize the text so that it will be a quick and easy read.

Target Message

I want my audience to know at least basic information on hang gliders and why we have it.
