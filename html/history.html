A Brief History

Why do we have hang gliders?

Well... It's because humans wanted to fly!

When did this start?

Humans tried to fly many many years ago...

Let's start with a little timeline!

  • 1799 - basic design for modern hang gliders were created
  • 1853 - first successful glide (a few hundred feet)
  • 1948 - para-wing (a flexible kite) was invented
  • 1948 - Mylar (a very light and strong plastic) was developed
  • 1961 - first true hang glider created by Thomas Purcell
  • 1971 - United States Hang Gliding Association formed
  • 1970s - hang gliders became a popular sport!
