Where Can I Glide?

If you have the equipment, you can basically hang glide anywhere that has big open areas. If not, you can find a school or instructor at the USHPA (U.S Hang Gliding and Paragliding Association) website. Click here to go to the website!

Are you scared to hang glide by yourself?

Don't worry! There is something called Tandem Gliding, which is where you can glide with an instructor. While they control everything, you can experience gliding!

Do you want to glide alone?

Wait, stop! You need to be at least 16 to try hang gliding alone.

Why?

It's not really a rule, but you are responsible for yourself while up in the air. No one can help you, so you will need to make your own decisions. That's why most places say you need to be at least 16 to hang glide alone.
